About

Interpolation is a band that prides itself on its eclectic background of musical influences. Being mainly rooted in jazz culture, we look to use the traditional formula of interpolating popular songs of our contemporary times with musical arrangements that reflect our backgrounds — Afrobeats, Salsa, Jazz, Flamenco, R&B and Soul influences.

No matter what type of event you have, we can interpolate any songs to create the perfect musical experience catered to your specific event.
